Jaipur

Jaipur is also known as the Pink City, due to the dominant color scheme of its buildings. It is located 268 km (167 miles) from the national capital New Delhi.

Pokhara

One of the most beautiful yet low budget natural destination in our neighbouring country Nepal. Famous for mountain villages and lakes.

The Himalayas

Mystery, beauty and tradition are ingrained into the great lands of the Himalaya. While the ancient monasteries of Tibet emit a calming peace, Kathmandu hums with the life of its people. Meanwhile, the behemoth of Mount Everest stands guard over an immense natural landscape of forests, gorges and peaks.

Maldives

The Maldives is 99% water and just one percent land. And as one might expect, the thrills of the Maldives lie on, under or over the surface of the water. Wherever you choose to holiday in the Maldives, there is watersports on offer.
